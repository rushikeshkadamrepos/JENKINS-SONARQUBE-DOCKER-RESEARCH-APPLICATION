Mood Mapper

Recognition:selected for the Zonal Round at Aavishkar, the Inter-Collegiate/Institute/Department Research Convention organized by the University of Mumbai and VESIT in December 2023.

Tools Used: Front End: Streamlit (Python), Backend: Python with machine learning libraries, Database: SQLite, Languages: Python,IDE: Sublime Editor, Command Prompt, JupyterLab

Mood Mapper is an innovative web application designed to decode sentiments conveyed in text. Leveraging natural language processing and machine learning techniques, Mood Mapper predicts and analyzes user emotions across various communication platforms. From social media moderation to customer feedback analysis, market research surveys, product review platforms, and even psychometric analysis, Mood Mapper offers a versatile solution. At the prestigious Aavishkar, the Inter-Collegiate/Institute/Department Research Convention organized by the University of Mumbai and VESIT in December 2023, our team proudly represented Mood Mapper. Selected for the Zonal Round, I actively contributed to the presentation process, explaining the intricacies of our project to the panelists. I'm grateful for the opportunity to have participated in such a prestigious event.
